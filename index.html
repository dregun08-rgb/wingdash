🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  |  App: Wing Dash Rating: 4+

Wing Dash is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Game scores and personal best — Stored using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.
  • Player username — If you choose to enter a name for the leaderboard, it is stored locally on your device.

Shared for the Global Leaderboard (Optional)

  • If you submit a score to the global leaderboard, your chosen username and score are stored anonymously. No personal information (email, device ID, IP address) is associated with this entry.
  • Submitting your score is entirely optional — you may skip it at any time.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA / IDFV)
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to third parties
  • Payment or financial information
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access

Third-Party Services

Wing Dash does not integrate with any third-party advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media SDKs. The app uses Apple's Game Center for native leaderboard support, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Wing Dash is rated 4+ and is appropriate for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The leaderboard requires only an optional, anonymous username with no account registration.

Data Retention

  • Local game data is retained until you uninstall the app.
  • Global leaderboard entries (anonymous username + score) are retained to preserve leaderboard history.

Your Rights

You may delete local data by uninstalling the app. To request removal of a leaderboard entry, contact us at hdgoldrush@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
